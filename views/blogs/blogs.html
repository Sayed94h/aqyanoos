Who is a good programmer?

The qualities of a good programmer

  • Learn fast
  • Flexible
  • Stress resistant
  • Creative, Positive mindset, think possible
  • Open to new ideas
  • Open to suggestions and critics
  • Learn from mistakes

Let me explain those qualities in details

Because the world of programming is changing so fast and almost everyday there come many new technologies, many improvements, better solutions,... a programmer should learn fast, be ok with learning new things and be able to adapt in different situations/circumstances or be flexible.

Being able to handle stress is very important becuase you as a programmer are dealing with many errors, bugs, problems, critics from your boss/supervisor,...

A programmer should be creative to build very user friendly applications, to the point applications and applications that do not confuse users. There are many applications that users can not find there ways in them, can not find what they are looking for, users do not find the application interesting,... A good programmer should think possible, have positive mindset and say YES to most of ideas he/she gets from his/her clients/boss/supervisor.

A good programmer should be ok with critics and suggestions from everyone, be a good listener, react rationally, act smart,...learn from his/her mistakes. It is super important that a programmer learns from his/her mistakes. If a programmer learns from his/her mistakes then he/she will become a great programmer or developer very fast and many companies will need him/her.

In conclusions

It is all about developments, improvements, solutions,...

It is all becuase of programmers that we have these facilities today. If songs sound interesting and better than the past, it is becuase of programmers. If health sectors are advanced, it is becuase of programmers. All these advanced technologies are becuase of programmers. That is why those qualities up there are very important for every programmer.
